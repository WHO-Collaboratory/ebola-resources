# Ebola Resources — WHO Collaboratory

A curated, community-maintained collection of resources supporting the response to the 2026 DRC Ebola Bundibugyo virus disease outbreak. This site is maintained by the **Ebola Community of Practice** within the [WHO Collaboratory](https://github.com/WHO-Collaboratory).

## Suggest a resource

The easiest way to contribute is to **open a GitHub Issue** using our
[New Resource template](https://github.com/bafnaprincy/ebola-resources/issues/new?template=new-resource.yml).
Just fill in the form — no coding or YAML knowledge needed. A maintainer will add it to the site.

See [Contributing](contributing.md) for more details.

## What you'll find here

Resources are organised into seven sections covering the breadth of the response:

| Section | What's covered |
|---|---|
| [Dashboards](dashboards.md) | Live epidemiological dashboards and situation trackers |
| [Epi Parameters](epi-parameters.md) | Epidemiological parameter databases, R packages, and review tools |
| [Outbreak Size Estimates](outbreak-size-estimates.md) | Modelling reports estimating the scale of the outbreak |
| [Risk of Spread](risk-of-spread.md) | Assessments of domestic and international spread risk |
| [Mobility Data](mobility-data.md) | Population movement and mobility datasets for the DRC |
| [Humanitarian Data](humanitarian-data.md) | Conflict, displacement, infrastructure, and other humanitarian datasets |
| [Therapeutics & Vaccines](therapeutics-vaccines.md) | WHO guidance on vaccines and therapeutics for BVD |
