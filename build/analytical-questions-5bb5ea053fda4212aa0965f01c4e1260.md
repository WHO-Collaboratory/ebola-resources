# Analytical questions 

This section collects key analytical questions relevant to the 2026 DRC Ebola Bundibugyo outbreak response.[(Click here for access)](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1iSZ6-BsTrQnOHvX60Q7eFAjCrqRLp559dFdKzsGj2Rw/edit?gid=0#gid=0)

# Background 

The questions were compiled with input from individuals with operational and analytical experience in country-level response. No formal consensus or agreement process was conducted beyond these individual consultations. As such, the questions are intended to help guide and coordinate analytical efforts while reflecting suggestions from the wider community.[(Click here for access)](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1iSZ6-BsTrQnOHvX60Q7eFAjCrqRLp559dFdKzsGj2Rw/edit?gid=0#gid=0)
